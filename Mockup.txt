The Basics
Clicking above a level makes the elevator move to it and pick up all the
people waiting.
Dropping people at the level with the indicator's color to win time.
An unattended person will give up after a given time and make a life will be lost.


The Conditions
The objective is to last as long as possible without letting the lives run out.
The bars in the middle of the game show the indicators of the people in the
elevators, which has a max capacity of 3 people.
